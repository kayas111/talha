About Kayas

Kayas is a platform designed to connect or link various students with similar interests in a business aspect where potentials buyers of items are respectively connected to sellers of the items within a period of less than 48 hours.

The platform serves to offer a service to those who have interest and intensions of acquiring flat screen televisions and woofers as well as those who intend to market off their televisions or woofers.
This is achieved through the connection of the two parties within the shortest time possible.

The platform is intended to serve students and for this reason,one needs to posses a valid University ID.

How it works

1.The party that intends to market off a television or woofer takes 1 to 3 clear photos of the item and the photos are sent for upload on the platform's display page.

NOTE: The following details are required from the seller; name of seller,student ID number,telephone contact,1 to 3 photos of the item,size of tv screen and price of the item.

2. The potential buyer who is interested in an item clicks the contact link adjascent to the item.

NOTE: The following details are required from the buyer; name of buyer,student ID number and telephone contact.

NB: Both parties are advised to verify each others details and therefore are advises to have their IDs with them during the transaction.
